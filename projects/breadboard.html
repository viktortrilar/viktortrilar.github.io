Project

Breadboard Circuit Verification

PyTorch · YOLOv8 · Blender · NIT Sendai

Automated verification of student breadboard circuits. A heading-aware DQN wire tracer and YOLOv8 component detection, trained on synthetic data generated in Blender, drive a bilingual FastAPI web app. Developed during a research exchange at NIT Sendai, Japan, under a JSPS grant.

Pipeline

Stage 01

Board Detection

Locate and rectify the breadboard within the input image to establish a canonical working frame.

Stage 02

Wire Segmentation

Segment connecting wires from the board and component clutter to isolate conductive paths.

Stage 03

DQN Wire Tracing

A heading-aware deep reinforcement learning agent traces each wire endpoint to endpoint across the segmentation mask.

Stage 04

Component Detection

YOLOv8 detects and classifies electronic components and their breadboard pin positions.

Stage 05

Topology Validation

Reconstruct the circuit netlist from traced wires and detected components, then validate against the expected topology.
